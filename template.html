Abstract

We present CAMIA, a novel approach for camera-aware image anonymization that preserves privacy while maintaining utility for machine learning applications.

Our method addresses the critical challenge of protecting individual privacy in image datasets while preserving the statistical properties necessary for effective machine learning model training. CAMIA introduces camera-aware techniques that account for different camera characteristics and imaging conditions when applying anonymization transformations.

By considering camera-specific properties, our approach ensures consistent anonymization quality across diverse imaging scenarios while maintaining the underlying data distribution that is crucial for machine learning performance. We demonstrate that CAMIA achieves superior privacy protection compared to existing methods while preserving model accuracy across various computer vision tasks.
